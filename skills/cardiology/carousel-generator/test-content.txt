# 5 Warning Signs of Heart Attack
Know these symptoms that could save your life

---

## 1. Chest Discomfort
• Pressure or squeezing feeling
• Center of chest
• Lasts more than a few minutes
• May come and go

---

## 2. Shortness of Breath
• With or without chest pain
• May occur before other symptoms
• Common first sign in women

---

## 3. Upper Body Pain
• Arms, back, neck, or jaw
• Often left arm
• Can feel like muscle strain

---

## 4. Cold Sweat & Nausea
• Breaking out in cold sweat
• Feeling sick to stomach
• Lightheadedness or dizziness

---

## 5. Don't Wait - Act FAST
• Time is muscle
• Call emergency services immediately
• Every minute counts for survival
