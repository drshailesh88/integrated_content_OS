## Content Strategy

### Video Length Targets
| Type | Length | Purpose |
|------|--------|---------|
| Shorts | 30-60 sec | Hooks, myths, quick facts |
| Standard | 8-12 min | Core educational content |
| Deep-dive | 15-25 min | Complex topics, full explanations |

### Upload Frequency Target
- **Ideal**: 2-3 videos/week
- **Minimum**: 1 video/week
- **Shorts**: 3-5/week

### Content Mix (Monthly)
- 40% Educational (pillars 1, 4)
- 25% Lifestyle (pillar 3)
- 20% Myth-busting (pillar 1)
- 15% Stories/Cases (pillar 5)

## Call-to-Action Framework

### Primary CTAs (Rotate)

2. **Comment**: "Apna sawaal comment mein likhiye"
3. **Share**: "Kisi apne ko bhejiye jinke kaam aa sake"

### Secondary CTAs
- Watch related video
- Download free resource (if applicable)
- Consultation booking (if offering)

### CTA Rules
- ONE primary CTA per video
- Place at natural pause points
- Never desperate or pushy
- Value-first framing ("Agar helpful laga...")

### Direct Competitors (Indian Medical YouTube)
